# Concept: Custom Tour

## Definition
A specialized service where customers can design their own travel itinerary in collaboration with [[Travery]] staff.

## Key Features
- **Personalized**: Points of interest, schedule, budget, and transportation are all customizable.
- **Collaborative**: Designed through direct [[Chat System]] with a [[Điều phối viên]].
- **Validity**: Design quotes are typically valid for 3 days.

## Workflow
1. **Request**: Customer sends desires (destination, dates, number of people, budget) via chat. Should be sent at least **7 days** before departure.
2. **Consultation**: [[Điều phối viên]] proposes a draft itinerary and price. This generated quote is valid for **3 days**.
3. **Refinement**: Both parties adjust the plan until satisfied.
4. **Finalization**: [[Điều phối viên]] creates the formal booking; customer pays 100%.
5. **Execution**: Follows standard tour operations with a assigned [[Hướng dẫn viên]].

## Constraints
- **Expiration**: If the customer does not approve the quote within 3 days, the booking transitions to `EXPIRED` and must be regenerated by the Coordinator due to potential price fluctuations.
