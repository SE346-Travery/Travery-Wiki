# Concept: Xe khách tuyến cố định (Fixed-route Bus)

## Definition
Inter-provincial bus services operated by [[travery]] on fixed routes and schedules between tourist destinations.

## Key Features
- **Seat Selection**: Customers pick specific seats from a layout (individual tickets, not full vehicle rental).
- **Payment**: 100% upfront; ticket is confirmed immediately without manual approval.
- **Luggage**: Up to 20kg free; oversized items or pets may be refused.

## Workflow
1. **Search**: Find trips by origin, destination, and date.
2. **Filter**: Filter by bus type (Sitting/Sleeper), time slot, floor, and row.
3. **Booking**: Select seats, provide contact info, and pay.
4. **Operation**: Driver and vehicle are pre-arranged; journey status is updated by staff.

## Constraints & Policies
- **Check-in**: Passengers must be at the pickup point 15-30 minutes before departure.
- **No Show-up Policy**:
    - The bus does **not wait** for missing passengers to ensure the schedule for others.
    - **No refund** is provided for no-shows.
- **Cancellation & Refund Policy**:
    - >2 hours before departure: 100% refund.
    - <2 hours before departure: 0% refund.
    - After departure: 0% refund.
    - If Travery cancels: 100% refund.
