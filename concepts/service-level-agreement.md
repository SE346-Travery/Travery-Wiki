# Concept: Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Travery defines specific response and processing times to ensure high-quality service and customer satisfaction.

## Support & Communication
- **Chat Response**: [[dieu-phoi-vien]] must respond within **30 minutes** during business hours (8:00–17:30, Monday to Saturday).
- **Negative Feedback**: Travery commits to responding to all negative reviews (1-2 stars) within **48 business hours**.

## Booking & Payments
- **Payment Hold**: For all services (Tour, Coach, Hotel), the system locks the selected spots/rooms for **15 minutes**. If payment is not completed within this window, the lock is released.
- **Tour Confirmation**: Manual verification by a [[dieu-phoi-vien]] is required for [[tour-tron-goi]], with a target confirmation time of **4 business hours** post-payment.
- **Hotel & Coach Confirmation**: Instant confirmation upon successful payment.

## Cancellations & Refunds
- **Refund Processing**: Once a cancellation is confirmed by a [[dieu-phoi-vien]], the refund is processed within **5-7 business days**.

## Custom Tours
- **Quote Validity**: Design proposals and price quotes for [[custom-tour]] are valid for **3 days**.
- **Request Lead Time**: Customers should submit custom requests at least **7 days** before the intended departure.
