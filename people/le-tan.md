# Role: Lễ tân (Receptionist)

## Description
Staff located at [[Khách sạn]] counters who manage guest stays.

## Responsibilities
- Verifying guest identification and confirming check-in/check-out.
- Activating and coordinating [[Add-On]] services.
- Finalizing invoices and collecting supplemental payments.
- Assisting guests with local inquiries.
