# Role: Quản trị viên (Admin)

## Description
System administrators who manage the [[Travery]] platform's internal operations.

## Responsibilities
- Managing internal accounts for staff ([[Điều phối viên]], [[Hướng dẫn viên]], [[Lễ tân]]).
- Configuring system permissions and roles.
- Reviewing business analytics and reporting.
- Overseeing overall system health.
