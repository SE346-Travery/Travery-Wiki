## Summary
This document provides an overview of **Travery** (Travel Discovery) as a self-operating travel enterprise. Unlike intermediary platforms, Travery directly owns and manages its core services: **Tour trọn gói** (Package Tours), **Xe khách tuyến cố định** (Fixed-route Buses), and **Khách sạn** (Hotels). 

It details:
- **Core Services**: Descriptions and payment terms for tours, buses, and hotels.
- **Stakeholders**: Roles and responsibilities of [[Khách hàng]], [[Điều phối viên]], [[Hướng dẫn viên]], and [[Lễ tân]].
- **Operational Processes**: Step-by-step procedures for booking, monitoring journeys, and handling emergencies.
- **Policies**: Comprehensive [[Chính sách hoàn tiền]] and [[No Show-up]] rules for each service type.
- **Custom Services**: The process for designing and booking [[Custom Tour]].
- **Service Commitments**: Standards for booking confirmation, transparency, and response times.
