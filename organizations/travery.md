# Organization: Travery

## Overview
**Travery** (Travel Discovery) is a self-operating travel enterprise that directly owns and manages its entire service ecosystem. Unlike many travel platforms that act as intermediaries, Travery maintains full control over service quality by managing its own tours, transportation, and accommodation facilities.

## Technical Infrastructure
Travery utilizes a modern tech stack and follows strict development guidelines:
- **Backend**: Spring Boot (Java)
- **Frontend**: Flutter
